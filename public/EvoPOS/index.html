About EvoStock

One Platform.
Every Business Type.

EvoStock is a modular inventory and point-of-sale management system built on .NET Core 8 and SQL Server — engineered to serve retail shops, pharmacies, supermarkets, hardware stores, and wholesalers with equal precision.

With 12 independently configurable feature modules, businesses activate only what they need — eliminating complexity while unlocking industry-specific power features like expiry/batch tracking for pharmacies, loyalty for retail, and multi-level categories for large-scale distributors.

Barcode POS Scanning GRN Stock Management Expiry & Batch Tracking Customer Loyalty Program Multi-Branch Operations Advanced Pricing Rules

Core Capabilities

12 Powerful Modules,
One Unified System

Every module is independently configurable — activate exactly what your business needs and grow from there.

Barcode POS

High-speed barcode scanning at the point of sale. Scan items to populate invoices instantly, support multiple barcode formats, and process sales with minimal keystrokes.

BARCODE Module

GRN & Stock Management

Goods Received Notes, supplier-linked purchase orders, stock-in workflows, and real-time stock level updates per product and branch. Full inventory lifecycle tracking.

Supply Chain Module

Expiry & Batch Tracking

Track product batches with manufacture/expiry dates. Near-expiry alerts, FEFO selling order enforcement, and batch-level traceability — critical for pharmacies and food retail.

BATCH_EXPIRY Module

Customer Loyalty

Points-based loyalty program with configurable earn rates, reward tiers, and redemption rules. Track customer purchase history, lifetime value, and segment by loyalty tier.

LOYALTY Module

Multi-Branch Management

Manage multiple store locations with centralised product masters, branch-specific stock levels, inter-branch transfers, and separate financial reporting per branch.

MULTI_BRANCH Module

Advanced Pricing Rules

Flexible pricing engine supporting quantity discounts, customer group pricing, promotional rates, seasonal pricing, and supplier cost-based margin rules.

PRICING_RULES Module

5-Level Product Categories

Hierarchical category trees with up to 5 levels — ideal for supermarkets (Beverages → Soft Drinks → Carbonated → Cola → 500ml) and complex product catalogues.

Core Module

Returns Management

Handle customer returns and supplier return-to-vendor workflows with automatic stock adjustments, credit note generation, and full audit trail per returned item.

RETURNS Module

Product Attributes

Custom product attributes like colour, size, weight, and formulation — enabling product variants, filtered search at POS, and detailed specification management.

PRODUCT_ATTRIBUTES Module

Advanced Reports

Sales, stock movement, purchase, profit & loss, supplier, and customer reports with date range filters, branch filters, and exportable formats for finance teams.

REPORTS Module

Brand & Supplier Management

Organise products by brand, link suppliers to products and purchase orders, track supplier pricing history, and manage supplier contact and payment terms centrally.

BRAND / SUPPLIER Modules

Why EvoStock

Built for Businesses That
Want More Control

From a single pharmacy counter to a multi-location supermarket chain — EvoStock adapts to your operational reality.

Faster Checkout Experience

Barcode scanning at POS reduces transaction time dramatically. No manual product searching — scan, total, pay, print. Customers are in and out faster.

Zero Stock Surprises

Real-time inventory tracking ensures you always know stock levels. Low-stock alerts, automatic reorder suggestions, and accurate count at every branch.

Compliance-Ready

Batch and expiry tracking keeps pharmacies and food retailers compliant. FEFO enforcement, expired stock lockouts, and batch traceability reports are built in.

Customer Retention Built In

Integrated loyalty program turns one-time buyers into repeat customers. Points, tiers, and targeted rewards are configured to match your retention strategy.

Pay for What You Use

Module-based architecture means you only activate — and pay for — the features your business needs. No bloated feature sets, no unnecessary complexity.

Data-Driven Decisions

12+ report types give managers the sales, stock, and supplier intelligence needed to make purchasing, pricing, and staffing decisions with confidence.

Scales With Your Growth

Add branches, products, users, and modules as your business grows — without changing systems. EvoStock grows with you from one shop to an enterprise chain.

Enterprise-Grade Security
