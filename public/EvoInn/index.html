About EvoInn

One Platform.
Total Hotel Control.

EvoInn is a modern, web-based hotel and reservation management platform that connects every department of your property — from the moment a guest makes a reservation to the instant they check out and the invoice is settled.

Built on Angular 17 with Microsoft SignalR real-time technology, EvoInn eliminates manual booking processes, reduces front desk errors, accelerates guest check-in, and gives management live operational visibility from a single dashboard.

Core Capabilities

Everything Your Hotel Needs,
In One System

From the first reservation to the final bill — EvoInn handles every operational touchpoint with precision and speed.

Reservation Management

Complete reservation lifecycle from enquiry to checkout. Create, edit, confirm, check-in, and check-out guests with a context-sensitive interface that adapts to every reservation stage.

Reservations Module

Live Management Dashboard

Real-time command center with 8 KPI cards, occupancy donut chart, revenue trend, room status grid, upcoming reservations timeline, and service usage analytics — all refreshing via SignalR.

Dashboard Module

Room Status Grid

Color-coded room tile grid grouped by floor. Click any tile for room details and quick actions: Check In, Check Out, Mark Maintenance, Assign Service — updated in real time.

Dashboard Module

Key Handling

Issue and return room keys with full timestamp tracking. Unreturned keys over 24 hours are flagged automatically. Real-time alerts notify management of key accountability issues.

Key Handling Module

Billing & Invoices

Comprehensive invoice management with room charges, services, meals, and extra charges. Add payments, view itemized breakdowns, print formatted invoices, and track balance due.

Billing Module

Daily Cashier Closing

End-of-day financial reconciliation with a full summary of cash, card, and online collections, refunds issued, net revenue, and formal day-close confirmation with audit record.

Cashier Module

Room & Rate Administration

Complete admin panel for room categories, facilities, room configurations (floor, status, facility assignments), rate plans (Daily, Seasonal, Special) with meal plan options.

Admin Module

Additional Services

Manage chargeable services like laundry, spa, meals, and transport with Fixed, Hourly, or Per-Usage pricing. Add services to reservations at any point during the guest stay.

Services Module

Live Alerts Panel

Scrollable real-time alert feed covering pending checkouts, unpaid invoices, unreturned keys, and overbooking situations — each with direct navigation to the relevant record.

Dashboard Module

Revenue Analytics

Daily revenue area chart, grouped bar chart showing room vs service vs meal revenue, occupancy donut, and service usage breakdown — across selectable date ranges.

Analytics Module

Multi-Method Payments

Record payments via Cash, Card, or Online transfer. Capture card type, reference number, and payment date. Track partial payments and outstanding balance to final settlement.

Billing Module
