About EvoDine

One Platform.
Total Restaurant Control.

EvoDine is a modern, web-based restaurant order management platform that connects every department of your restaurant — from the moment a customer sits down to the instant the bill is settled.

Built on Angular 17 with Microsoft SignalR real-time technology, EvoDine eliminates paper-based order-taking, reduces communication errors, accelerates order fulfilment, and gives management live operational visibility from a single dashboard.

See EvoDine

Every module purpose-designed for the real demands of restaurant operations.

app.evodine.com/dashboard

Management Dashboard

The operational command center. Managers access total revenue, active orders, table occupancy, average preparation time, and revenue trend charts — all refreshing in real time.

  • Configurable KPI cards with delta comparisons
  • Revenue trend & order distribution charts
  • Peak hours bar chart analysis
  • Top 8 selling items ranking
  • Payment method breakdown
app.evodine.com/order

Order Creation Module

Servers create digital orders directly at the table. Browse menu by category, select portions, add extras, attach special notes — then submit to instantly notify kitchen and bar.

  • Category-based menu browsing
  • Dine In, Takeaway, Delivery, Package types
  • Per-item add-ons and special notes
  • Pay Now or Pay Later options
  • Add more items to existing orders
app.evodine.com/order-movement

Kitchen & Bar Order Movement

The heart of kitchen and bar operations. Real-time order cards show all items, current stage, and action buttons. Chefs progress each item through the preparation stages with a single click.

  • Auto-routed: kitchen vs bar items
  • One-click stage advancement
  • Pin up to 3 priority orders
  • Bulk select and advance items
  • Table change and add-more features
app.evodine.com/cashier

Cashier & Payment Processing

Orders appear automatically in the cashier queue once every item is served. Cashiers apply charges, print formatted invoices, and process cash, card, or split payments with full validation.

  • Auto-populated billing queue
  • Toggle surcharges (fixed or percentage)
  • Formatted invoice printing
  • Cash, credit, debit payment
  • Split payment (Cash+Card, Card+Card)
app.evodine.com/admin/menu

Menu & Administration Setup

The administration panel gives full control over the restaurant's menu structure. Configure categories linked to service types, define portion sizes, set per-portion pricing, and manage addons with individual unit prices.

  • Category with order type linking
  • Multi-portion pricing per item
  • Food item images and combo flags
  • Addon management with pricing
  • Surcharge rules (fixed + percentage)
